A bit about us

→
Kimera is by creators, for creators.


We offer a discovery engine to empower the dreams of creators by connecting them with their fans authentically that no other platform can.

Our founding team is a scrappy group of writers, video creators, and innovators based in Cambridge, Massachusetts, as Harvard sophomores. We've spent most of 2021 venturing across the country to traverse the creator economy— from the coast of Oregon and the plains of Texas to the streets of LA and Boston.

Before Kimera, we were launching Boundless Media, a YouTube startup that filmed wild challenges and our travel around America.

This experience changed everything.

When we crashed our car after filming in a blizzard, we realized creators need a better way to connect with fans. The drive for discovery by "gaming the algorithm" through sensationalist content was unsustainable—and life-threatening.

And so Kimera was born: an engine to help aspiring creators achieve the dream we almost lost everything for. A dream to create stories that reach a passionate audience and build a relationship with fans to form the foundation of any creative career.

And at Kimera, we have never stopped creating—from vlogging our company's growth story on our YouTube channel to writing books on the creator economy.

As we build the future of creation, we never deviate from our First Principles: people over platforms, aligned incentives, and transparent simplicity—all under the notion that together we are boundless.
